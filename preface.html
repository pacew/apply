Welcome NEFFA Applicant,

  • General applications for the 2019 festival are now closed (the deadline was 11:59pm, EDT, October 3, 2018). We are still accepting applications for dance performances and ritual dance performances.
  • Submit one Event Application Form for EACH event you wish to propose.
  • Submit applications only for yourself or for groups where you are the designated leader. Do not submit an application for other performers or groups.
  • You may submit no more than 5 applications PER individual or group.
  • Please construct your sentences and spell your words carefully -- this information goes directly from your application to the Schedule Grid and Program Book.
  • Do not use all capital letters or all small letters, and avoid the use of abbreviations.
  • You will receive an email with a link to a copy of your submission.

We will send you instructions on how to enter and/or update your contact data and group information within several days of receiving your application.

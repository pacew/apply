Welcome NEFFA Applicant,

  • The deadline for applications is 11:59pm, EDT, October 2, 2018.
  • Submit one Event Application Form for EACH event you wish to propose.
  • Submit applications only for yourself or for groups where you are the designated leader. Do not submit an application for other performers or groups.
  • You may submit no more than 5 applications PER individual or group.
  • All applications for any one individual or group must be submitted at the same time. This helps us keep things organized!
  • Please be complete, with accurate spelling for ALL information since this information goes directly from your application to the Schedule Grid and Program Book.
  • Do not use all capital letters or all small letters, and avoid the use of abbreviations.
  • You will receive an email with a link to a copy of your submission.

IMPORTANT: Once we have received your application(s), within a few days, we will send you instructions on how to supply and/or update your contact data and group information.

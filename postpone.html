Greetings, NEFFA community!

We are delighted to announce that applications are now open for NEFFA 2022, our 78th year! The Festival will be held April 22-24, 2022. Application deadline is October 17, 2021 at 11:59 pm.

As of September, the Board is continuing to plan for an in-person Festival. The Board is committed to a safe Festival and is actively discussing policy for vaccination, testing, masks, social distancing, and other safety protocols.

We expect to make a decision no later than January 1, 2022 on whether to hold the Festival in person. If the in-person festival is cancelled, we will pivot to holding an online Festival. Applications include a question on whether performers are applying for in-person, online, or both. Either way, we encourage you to apply now, so that we can put together the best program as soon as possible after January 1.

When making your decision to apply for in-person, applicants should assume that vaccination and safety measures will be in place. NEFFA has no plans to attempt a hybrid Festival, so if the Festival goes forward in person, online-only applicants would not be on the program, and vice versa.

If held in person, the Festival will be held at the Best Western Royal Plaza Hotel & Trade Center, 181 Royal Plaza Dr West, Marlborough, MA 01752. For more information about our new venue, please see https://www.neffa.org/neffa-2022-festival-site/.

On behalf of the Program Committee and the NEFFA Board, we extend our heartfelt thanks for your support for this wonderful folk tradition that is the Festival.

Enthusiastically yours,

Lynn Noel
NEFFA Program Chair
